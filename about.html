About Us

We are Comet Robotics team 1885, formerly known as ILITE. We use everything we've learned in the classroom in a competitive setting with FRC Robotics. Five distinct sub-teams make up Comet Robotics' well-organized organizational structure. Build, programming, electronics, graphics, and outreach are some of these sub-teams. Additionally, we want to encourage a feeling of student leadership and direction to guide our teams to success.
