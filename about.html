ABOUT US
Located in Haymarket, Virginia, we are team 1885, Comet Robotics, formerly known as ILITE. With FRC Robotics, we apply everything we've learned in the classroom to a competitive environment. The team structure of Comet Robotics is well-organized and consists of multiple different sub-teams. Some of these sub-teams include outreach, electronics, build, programming, and graphics. In order to lead our teams to success, we also try to encourage a sense of student leadership and direction.
By teaching and motivating individuals to use the strength of teamwork to solve challenging problems, COMET Robotics hopes to achieve greatness. Furthermore, we anticipate teaching future generations the importance of robots in order to inspire them to think creatively and revolutionize robotics as a whole.
OUR MISSION
COMET Robotics aims to tackle problems by making in depth strategies and the use of cooperation to accomplish difficult tasks.
We reach out to multiple different schools to have STEAM nights to educate the youth about the importance of robotics by engaging them in simple, cognitive activities.
Together, we create a robot for competition and aim to win by means of innovation.
