ABOUT US

Team 1885 Comet Robotics (formerly ILITE) is located in Haymarket, Virginia. This season will be Comet's third year competing in the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC). Our team emphasizes student leadership and organization, as shown through our multiple sub-teams.

Comet challenges students to solve real-world problems and play active roles in their community. On the competition field, members apply their engineering skills to solve new challenges and promote the values of FIRST such as innovation, discovery, and Gracious Professionalism. Off the field, members inspire generations of future innovators by hosting outreach events and collaborating with other organizations.

OUR MISSION

Comet aims to tackle complex problems by creating in-depth strategies and innovative solutions in robotics.

We collaborate with local elementary schools to host STEAM nights, educating young engineers through creative and fun activities.

Together, we leave a lasting impact on the robotics community and inspire those in our home community.
