## First Day of Our STEAM Summer Camp

Our annual STEAM Summer Camps are one of our main ways to interact with the community.

Comet team members with multiple years of experience collaborate to create a fun and fruitful experience for youth from elementary to middle school. Lessons, activities and snacks are provided from 8AM-12PM to 1PM-5PM for a 5 day period. This is truly our teams favorite outreach event.
