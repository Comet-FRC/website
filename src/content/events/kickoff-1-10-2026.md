## FRC Kickoff: The Season Begins

FRC Kickoff marks the official start of the robotics season. Team members will gather to watch the live game reveal, review the rules, and begin brainstorming robot strategies.

This day sets the foundation for the entire season, shaping our design decisions, priorities, and overall direction moving forward.
