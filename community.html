Alvey STEAM Night

January 29, 2025

Comet Robotics proudly participated in the Alvey STEAM Night, for the second year in a row, a collaborative event with several local clubs such as BAAP, Science National Honor Society, Math Honor Society, STAR Club, and the National Honor Society. Together, we provided an engaging and interactive experience for students, showcasing the exciting world of robotics and STEAM. The event featured hands-on activities such as building with Snap Circuits, driving our robot, and creating personalized pins at the pin-making station. These activities, combined with informative demonstrations and discussions, offered students valuable insights into the fields of robotics, engineering, and technology. Our collaboration with these distinguished clubs helped foster a strong sense of community and encouraged future innovation in STEAM. 

Tyler STEAM Night

December 16, 2024

Comet Robotics was thrilled to host the Tyler STEAM Night for the second year at Tyler Elementary School in Haymarket, VA. This event was made possible through our collaboration with BAAP, the Science National Honor Society, the Math Honor Society, and the STAR Club. The night was filled with exciting hands-on activities designed to inspire and engage students in the world of robotics and engineering. Attendees had the opportunity to interact with our robot, drive it around, and participate in building projects using Snap Circuits. Additionally, a pin-making station allowed students to unleash their creativity. By working with these clubs from Battlefield High School, Comet Robotics was able to provide a dynamic and educational environment that not only showcased the potential of robotics but also emphasized the importance of teamwork and innovation in the STEAM field. We had about 150 attendees at this event, and our STEAM night was a huge hit. 

Gravely STEAM Night

April 17, 2024

For our Gravley STEAM Night, we’ve been attending for two years in a row now, and we are excited to go again this year. In 2024, we took our Crescendo robot for the first time to a STEAM night. It was a big hit with the elementary schoolers, as it shot out notes and everyone engaged with the idea of robotics. It was a fun way to showcase our team’s creativity and introduce the younger students to STEAM concepts in an interactive way. We look forward to continuing this tradition and making it even better this year.
